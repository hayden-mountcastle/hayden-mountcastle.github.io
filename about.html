Hi, I'm Hayden. I'm an epidemiologist and data analyst dedicated to advancing healthcare. I hold a B.S. in Biology from New York University Abu Dhabi and an MPH in Epidemiology from UCLA. Currently, I live in Boston and work in the Koenen Lab at the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health. My primarily focus is on NeuroGAP, a large cohort of over 40,000 participants, which seeks to understand genetic and epidemiologic factors contributing to psychosis and PTSD in East Africa. See 'Projects' for a closer look into some of the things I've been doing.

Outside of work, I love cooking, play basketball, travel the world, and learning new things. I'm engaged to the amazing and beautiful Joey Bui and together we spoil Simba, our little Bichon-Shih Tzu mix.
